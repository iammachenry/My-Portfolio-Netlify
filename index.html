Featured Project | Sales & Service Cloud

Global Grocery: Enterprise Sales & Service Cloud Implementation

Full Presentation
global-grocery.com.ng

Business Context

A multi-regional grocery retail chain expanding to 3 new territories needed unified visibility into sales performance and customer support operations. Leadership couldn't track forecasts or measure team performance across regions, creating risk in expansion decisions.

The Challenge

  • Sales data scattered across spreadsheets (no real-time pipeline visibility)
  • Support cases tracked manually (average resolution time: 3.5 days)
  • Forecast accuracy below 60% (leadership couldn't plan confidently)
  • No consistent process for expansion (each region operated differently)

My Solution

I set up an integrated Sales Cloud and Service Cloud system with automated lead routing, real-time forecasting dashboards, and SLA-based case management. This gave leadership complete visibility and created consistent processes across all regions.

Key Implementation Details

  • Unified data model with territory-based security (enabled cross-region reporting while protecting sensitive data)
  • Automated lead scoring and routing so hot leads reached the right rep within minutes
  • Real-time executive dashboards that gave leadership visibility into forecasts, pipeline, and support performance
  • SLA tracking with early warnings to prevent violations before they happened
  • User training and change management to ensure adoption across 50+ users in 4 departments
